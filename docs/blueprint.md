# **App Name**: VVIOK Industry Digital Hub

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: A visually appealing homepage showcasing key filtration products, industries served, and company strengths.
- Product Catalog: Detailed catalog of filtration products, categorized by type (air, liquid, industrial, customized) with descriptions and inquiry options.
- Industry Solutions Overview: Dedicated pages for each industry served, highlighting relevant filtration solutions and their benefits.
- Manufacturing & Quality Assurance: A detailed overview of VVIOK Industry's manufacturing processes, quality control measures, and compliance with international standards to build trust.
- Global Export Information: Specific details about the company's export capabilities, including countries served, packaging, logistics, and international quality certifications.
- Inquiry & Contact System: Contact form, address, phone, email, Google Maps integration, and a WhatsApp inquiry button for easy communication.
- SEO Content Enhancement: Generative AI tool analyzes page content for SEO optimization based on target keywords, suggesting improvements to headings and copy.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Industrial Blue (#20639B) to convey trust and reliability.
- Background color: Light Grey (#F0F4F8) for a clean and professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Slate Grey (#6B7A8F) for secondary actions and highlights, offering a subtle contrast.
- Headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif, with a modern look, is appropriate for headlines.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that combines a modern look and a little warmth or personality, suitable for body text.
- Use a consistent set of professional icons to represent product categories and industry applications.
- A clean, grid-based layout with ample whitespace for easy readability and navigation.
- Subtle animations, such as loading spinners and smooth transitions, to enhance user experience without being distracting.
